Loading Croc: Legend of the Gobbos (PS1)

Track 1 is split into parts due to file-size limits. This page must download and stitch Track 1 before the PS1 emulator can boot.
Input will feel dead during loading. That is normal.
When the game appears, click it once to focus keyboard input.
If you see a menu instead of the game, the disc did not mount (usually a missing file or a filename mismatch).
